Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Every feature earns its place. Cole POS is fast by design — no bloated menus, no unnecessary steps.

Fast checkout

One-tap products, instant totals, numeric keypad for cash. Ring up an order in under ten seconds.

Smart inventory

Track by unit or weight, set low-stock thresholds, scan barcodes. Alerts before you run out.

Analytics

Revenue, order volume, average sale size, and top products — all on one screen, always current.

Multi-role access

Admin, Manager, and Cashier roles with granular permissions. Staff see exactly what they need.

How it works

Up and running
in minutes.

1

Pick your business type

Choose Coffee Shop, Grocery, or Restaurant. Harbor loads the right layout, catalog structure, and checkout flow automatically.

2

Add your products

Import a CSV or build your catalog item by item. Set prices, categories, tax rates, and stock levels. Weighted and barcode items supported.

3

Start selling

Open the register, tap products, select payment, and charge. Every order is tracked and analytics update in real time.
