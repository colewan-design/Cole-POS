# Cole POS — Demo Video Script

**Target length:** ~3 minutes (each scene lists a duration; trim/extend as needed)
**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover (talking-head optional), light background music
**Audience:** Prospective small-business owners (café, grocery, restaurant, nail salon) evaluating the app

---

## Before you hit record

1. **Seed realistic demo data first.** Empty tables/carts/charts look broken on camera. Place a handful of test orders in each business mode you plan to show, and make sure Products/Inventory/Customers/Suppliers/Employees each have a few rows.
2. **Pick your recording tool.** For a quick, no-install option on Windows: `Win + G` opens Xbox Game Bar's recorder. For more control (multiple scenes, cursor highlighting, mic mixing), use OBS Studio.
3. **Browser setup:** maximize the window, 100% zoom, hide the bookmarks bar and any extensions toolbar, use a clean profile with no notification popups.
4. **Skip `/diagnostics`** — it's the developer telemetry page, not customer-facing.
5. **Minimize business-mode switching.** Changing modes happens on the Settings page and re-flavors the whole app, so batch your takes: record everything you need in Coffee Shop mode, switch once, record everything in Restaurant mode, etc., then reorder the clips in your editor to match the script below.
6. Have your **product URL** ready for the final on-screen CTA.

## Scene-by-scene

### 1. Cold open — Landing page hero (0:00–0:12)
**Screen:** Marketing landing page, hero section (`#` / lp-hero).
**Action:** Let the particle-field background animate a beat, then a slow cursor move across the hero.
**Voiceover:**
> "Meet Cole POS — one point-of-sale app that becomes a different system depending on what you run."

**On-screen text (optional lower-third):** *One app. Four businesses.*

---

### 2. The pitch (0:12–0:22)
**Screen:** Scroll to the Highlights section of the landing page.
**Voiceover:**
> "Café, grocery store, restaurant, or nail salon — Cole POS reshapes its register, catalog, and reports to match your business. Checkout in under ten seconds."

---

### 3. Log in → Dashboard (0:22–0:42)
**Action:** Click **Log in**, sign in, land on `/dashboard`.
**Screen:** Dashboard metric cards, then click through the range selector (Today / Week / Month / All).
**Voiceover:**
> "Every account opens on a live dashboard — sales, orders, and trends at a glance, filtered by any time range you need."

---

### 4. Register walkthrough — Coffee Shop mode (0:42–1:15)
**Action:**
1. Navigate to `/register`. Show the top bar: date/time pills, **Start Shift** with an opening-cash amount.
2. Tap through the category tabs (icon-topped cards, colored tag pills).
3. Add 2–3 products to the cart.
4. Point out the order panel: customer name/order number header, promo field, payment method pill.
5. Tap **Place Order** → Payment Sheet opens → select **Cash** → tap a quick-cash amount → confirm.

**Voiceover:**
> "Start your shift, ring up an order in a few taps, and take payment — cash, card, or e-wallet — without leaving the screen."

---

### 5. Track Order panel (1:15–1:28)
**Action:** Tap **Track Order** to open the floating status strip. Click a card to advance it (On Kitchen Hand → To Be Served → All Done).
**Voiceover:**
> "Orders in flight stay visible right on the register, so staff always know what's next."

---

### 6. Restaurant mode — tables & order types (1:28–1:50)
*(Recorded after switching Business Mode to Restaurant in Settings — see prep note above.)*
**Action:** Register in restaurant mode: open the **Select Table** dropdown, pick a table; open the order-type dropdown (dine-in/takeout/delivery).
**Voiceover:**
> "Switch to restaurant mode and the register grows a table map and order-type controls automatically — no separate app, same login."

---

### 7. Quick-cut — Grocery & Nail Salon modes (1:50–2:10)
**Action:**
- Grocery: show the barcode field ("Scan or enter barcode") and a weighted-item badge on a product card.
- Nail Salon: show the services search bar and service cards.

**Voiceover:**
> "Running a grocery? Scan a barcode or key in weighted items. Running a salon? Browse services instead of SKUs. Same app, four different personalities."

---

### 8. Reports (2:10–2:35)
**Action:** Navigate to `/reports`. Cycle the range selector, then pan across the chart cards: Payment Breakdown (donut), Business Mode Totals, Top Selling Lines, Recent Orders, Shift Snapshot / Shift History.
**Voiceover:**
> "Owners get a full reporting suite — payment mix, top sellers, shift totals and history — no spreadsheet exports required."

---

### 9. Back-office montage (2:35–2:52)
**Action:** Quick 2–3 second cuts through: Products, Inventory, Orders, Customers, Suppliers, Employees.
**Voiceover:**
> "Behind the register: full product and inventory management, customer profiles, suppliers, and staff — all in one place."

---

### 10. Settings — branding & mode switch (2:52–3:05)
**Action:** Open Settings, show the business name field and the Business Mode dropdown (the same one used to switch modes earlier).
**Voiceover:**
> "Rebrand it with your business name, and switch modes any time your business does."

---

### 11. Outro / CTA (3:05–3:20)
**Action:** Cut back to the landing page CTA section / sign-up form.
**Voiceover:**
> "No card required, free during early access. Get started at [your-domain]."

**On-screen text:** *One app. Four businesses. Free during early access.*

---

## Notes for editing
- Suggested music: upbeat, unobtrusive corporate/lo-fi bed, ducked under voiceover.
- Keep cursor movement deliberate and slow — fast mouse-flailing reads poorly on camera.
- If trimming to 60–90 seconds for social, keep scenes 1, 4, 8, and 11 and cut the rest.
